Frequently Asked Question

What is the F$?
F$ stands for funbucks, a type of currency used in some classrooms.
Where do you get F$?
You can find funbucks at local toy shops, just ask for play money. You can also find them on amazon, by looking up "funbucks."
What is my money/funbucks going to be used for?
Your money will be used to pay for supplies, and your funbucks will be distributed between the staff team.
